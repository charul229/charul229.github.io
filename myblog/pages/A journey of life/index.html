A journey of life

A ton of questions knock my head when I ponder over the most familiar word Life. A big question that is still bothering me is 'What is life, where do we belong? Do our existence is limited to the time period we live on this earth or it is somthing else that we are not aware of'? What if it is true whatever is written in ancient texts, Life is a compilation of numerous small journeys strung together. A life is a small portion of a big journey? Or Does it start with the birth and end with the death? This is a big question that is still unanswered. It is generally told that even the demigods doesnt know the mystrery of life and death. Why is it like that? Life is really a big mystery which noone knows how will it unfold?
A life starts and it ends. It doesnt ask from us when should it be started and when it should end. It seems to be controlling us completely without offering us any free will. A sanskrita sloka from Srimad Bhagavad gita which explains the cycle of birth and death which we are in from thousands of years and we dont know when it will end. When i do see myself and others, we are running behind something each and every moment. We never stop thinking achieving something that will make us happy even momentarily. I am trying to understand myself. What is the suitable way or path to follow so that we can live this life as it is expected. Can you imagine a situation, where you just took a birth. Do your existence has no meaning on earth? Do you find your meaning when you learn something? Who knows how long a life will go on? To ...Why the opening of the article is started with dots, if you noticed then you really understand why the title of the article is chosen such that. Our journey hasnt been started with this life. We have lived tons of life already and got birth again. how should it be? Who will decide? These questions everyone should keep asking. When will this life end, noone knows. So better to know the idea of observing yourself time to time, can ascend you the staircase of life one step ahead. Get on the first step of knowing yourself. So to say, explaining our journey of life can be very confusing. As we can not express our experiences in words sometimes. Even we maybe forgetting the moments, a part of us will be living it one way or the other. My thoughts that come from a different paths and make me realise the importance of the things that i avoided before. It ensure me also that it is the right time to understand these things.

What we are here for?

This is really interesting to notice that we are burdened with the ideas then how can we live a life peacefully. At least there is some free will on what we can think about. What brought you here? The understanding of being here tell you something about your resposibility. Does it encourage you for taking a proper action? What is it that keep you alive, keep you motivated in doing whatever you are doing? There are tonnes of questions that we can ask from ourself, the interesting part is, what action we take to understand that question and do some brainstorming to come up to something valuable. Does it really worth asking these questions and spending some time to gather some thoughts to reach to some valuable form of understanding. We hardly find people to encourage us taking part in these kind of small detour from our normal journey of life. Let me tell you, it is worth time spending to explore these uncharted areas because who knows what we can found out.
