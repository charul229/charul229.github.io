A journey of life

....Why the opening of the article is started with dots, if you noticed then you really understand why the title of the article is chosen such that. Our journey hasnt been started with this life. We have lived tons of life already and got birth again. how should it be? Who will decide? These questions everyone should keep asking. When will this life end, noone knows.
