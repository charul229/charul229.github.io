About

I am a research scholar in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, IIT Hyderabad. I am currently working on the problem on "a moving contact lines dynamics" with Dr. Harish N. Dixit and Dr. Lakshamana D. Chandrala. My area of interest has been in fluid dynamics problems. I believe I have developed some expertises in experimental tactics and understanding of the theoretical constructs of the problems. My home town is Alwar, Rajasthan. I finished all my schooling there only. I finished my bachelor from NIT Allahabad. I finished my masters from NIT Warangal in Thermal and Fluid Engineering. I am much interested in reading books.
