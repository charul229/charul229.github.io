Hello! I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, IIT Hyderabad. I am working on a problem "a study of flow dynamics occurring very near a moving contact line" with Dr. Harish N. Dixit and Dr. Lakshamana D. Chandrala. The moving contact line is studied in great detail with the help of experimental observations and theoretical constructs. An experimental technique, PIV is used to explore the flow dyanmics happening near a contact line. For more details, check out a pre-print of a recently submitted paper in the publication tab above. Moreover, i am interested to work in a wide range of fluid dynamics problems. Other than PIV tehcnique, I have explored other useful optical techniques like shadowgraphy, schlieren imaging, holography, interferometry etc.
